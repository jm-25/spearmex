Triton Catamaran

Introducing Our Custom Designed 50-Foot Catamaran

We are thrilled to present our brand-new custom-designed 50-foot catamaran, equipped with powerful twin 430hp Caterpillar engines. This vessel boasts a cruising speed of 15 knots and can reach a maximum speed of 25 knots when at full capacity, making it one of the fastest boats in its category.

Experience ultimate comfort with our fully shaded lounge seating area on the main deck, along with a similar layout on the flybridge. The open sky deck is perfect for sun lovers, offering a separate bar for refreshing drinks.

Our unique grill-based menu features an Ocean & Farm-to-Table Taco Party, complemented by expertly crafted mixology cocktails. This exceptional culinary experience is designed to elevate your time on board.

The catamaran is fully equipped with a range of water sports equipment for endless fun, including paddleboards, kayaks, giant floating mats, soft tops, snorkeling gear, fishing tackle, and WaveRunners (Seadoo).

Join us for an unforgettable journey on the water, where luxury meets adventure!
